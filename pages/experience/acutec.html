Project: Packaging Configuration Automation

One of my primary projects involved developing a software solution to automate the packaging configuration process. Prior to my internship, the packaging specifications for each of the 12,000+ SKUs were managed manually, resulting in significant time investment and occasional errors.

My solution involved:

  • Analyzing the existing packaging requirements and identifying patterns
  • Developing an algorithm to automatically determine optimal packaging configurations based on product specifications
  • Creating a user-friendly interface for managing exceptions and special cases
  • Integrating the solution with existing inventory management systems
  • Conducting thorough testing to ensure reliability

The resulting system not only saved approximately 1,000 hours annually but also improved packaging consistency and reduced material waste.
