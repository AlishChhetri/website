Data Gators

Computer and Information Science Department, Allegheny College | September 2024 - May 2025

Role Overview

As a member of Data Gators at Allegheny College's Computer and Information Science Department, I collaborate with peers to promote data science education and engage students through hands-on learning experiences. Data Gators is a student-led initiative that aims to make data science more accessible and applicable to real-world problems.

Key Contributions

  • Mentored students in data science through hands-on learning experiences and interactive workshops
  • Developed an educational website as an online resource for learning data science, featuring structured lessons and practical applications
  • Created and facilitated data analysis workshops that bridge theoretical concepts with practical applications
  • Collaborated with faculty to integrate data science principles into the broader computer science curriculum
  • Designed and implemented learning activities that make complex data science concepts more approachable for beginners

Technical Skills Applied

  • Data Analysis: Using Python, R, and other tools to analyze and interpret data
  • Data Visualization: Creating clear, insightful visualizations to communicate findings
  • Statistical Methods: Applying appropriate statistical techniques to derive insights
  • Web Development: Building educational resources and interactive learning platforms
  • Technical Communication: Explaining complex data science concepts in accessible terms

Impact and Educational Outcomes

My involvement with Data Gators has contributed to the data science community at Allegheny College:

  • Increased interest and participation in data science activities among students from diverse backgrounds
  • Created sustainable educational resources that continue to benefit new students
  • Fostered a collaborative learning environment where students can explore data science together
  • Provided practical, hands-on experience that complements classroom learning
  • Helped students develop the skills and confidence to pursue data science projects and careers
